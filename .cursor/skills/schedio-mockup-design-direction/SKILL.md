# Schedio Mockup Design Direction

This repo needs one coherent product family with a clear client dashboard, a linked intake flow, and two internal governance routes.

## Shared tone

- Trustworthy
- Operational
- Engineering-grade
- Reviewable

## Route personalities

- `/`: verification-first, operational, inventory-led
- `/create-package`: task-flow focused, reviewable, calmer than the dashboard
- `/review-workbench`: internal, analytical, file-first drafting
- `/review-console`: internal, controlled, authority-first approval

## Rules

- Keep the interfaces intentional and presentation-ready.
- Avoid generic SaaS polish that makes every route feel interchangeable.
- Use spacing, information density, and copy tone to create distinction.
- Make human oversight visible where it matters.
- Preserve the sense that original filenames stay visible and auditable throughout the flow.

# Schedio Mockup Design Direction

## Shared tone

- Trustworthy
- Operational
- Engineering-grade
- Reviewable

## Route personalities

- `portal-trust`: calm, guided, reassuring
- `portal-operations`: denser, more visible workflow state
- `review-console`: internal, controlled, traceability-first

## Rules

- Keep the interfaces presentation-ready.
- Use spacing, information density, and copy tone to create distinction.
- Make human oversight visible where it matters.
- Preserve the sense that originals are retained and Ignite is the downstream handoff.
